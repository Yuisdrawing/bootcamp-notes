Hot Tips for Better Pasta

by Vizual Criminal

  • Salt your water like the sea.
  • Add some pasta water.
  • Bring water to a rapid boil before adding the pasta.
  • Drain don’t dry.
  • Add pasta to sauce — not the other way around.
